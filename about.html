Values/Standards

Be Transparant

Trust is the foundation of any relationship. That's why I like to be honest about everything in the project, including pricing and expectations, so my clients feel comfortable throughout the process.

Be Kind

My clients are great people to work with. I will always treat them with the kindess and the respect they deserve. It costs nothing to be kind and I think everyone deserves to be treated fairly.

People Not Profits

The success of a project is measured by positive experiences not how much money I can get out of a client. I want to help people, not take advantage of them.

Keep Learning

It's easy to stop learning once you've finished school. I think people should always try to enrich their lives with knowledge and passion. I try to learn from my clients as much as they might learn about the design process from me.

Quality not Quantity

If I want to provide a positive experience then I can't just churn out work. That's why I work on only a select few client at a time to ensure my clients have my full attention and creative effort.

Work To Live

Work is only one part of my life, and I aim to keep it that way. When we prioritise our families and passions we're healthier and happier people. I try to keep that in mind as I foster creative passion in others.

Always Ask Questions

I want to create the best solution I can for my clients and that starts by asking questions. If I don't understand the project then how can I be expected to deliver something they're happy with?

Keep It Simple

Simplicity means clarity. If it's clear then it's understandable. I try to keep evertything as simple as possible. That means I won't bore clients with technical jargon or design an elaborate concept I can't actually complete. Many of best things in the world are, at their core, simple.

I like to listen. I have learned a great deal from listening carefully. Most people never listen.

Ernest Hemingway
